Frequently Asked Questions

Gardening is rewarding, but it can be inconvenient. We've come up with a unique kit that aims to solve all of the following problems with indoor home gardening:

  • TIME
    • People today have fast-paced and busy lifestyles, making it difficult to find the time to do gardening. Agroage will provide you with everything you need to grow your own delicious produce, delivered to your door. Agroage will also provide several ways for you to make sure you never forget to water your plants.
  • SPACE
    • Big bags of soil and bulky plant pots can take up a lot of space in your home. Agroage will provide you with the optimal amount of soil, compact plant pots, and other materials to grow your favourite produce, with a small physical footprint.
  • MONEY
    • Purchasing different varieties of seeds and growing materials can be expensive. Agroage is committed to providing you with changing selections of many different seeds without blowing a hole in your budget.
  • KNOWLEDGE
    • Every plant is different, so it can be difficult to properly care for them. Agroage will provide you specific care instructions that are tried-and-true to grow different varieties of plants, including unique virtual content and personal support from expert gardeners.

Here's what you can expect to see in an Agroage indoor home gardening kit:

SEEDS

Your selection of seeds from a variety of offerings

SOILS

The optimal amount of soils, organic fertilizers, and nutrient solutions required to grow your selected seeds

CONTAINERS

Compact sprouting jars, microgreen trays, and plant pots to grow your selected seeds

ACCESSORIES

Your selection from a variety of accessories like our aesthetic plant pot sleeves

TOOLS

Indoor gardening tools like spray bottles, self-watering bulbs, and more

INSTRUCTIONS

Specialized plant care instructions printed on recycled paper, with bonus virtual content

RECIPES

Suggested recipes that incorporate your selected plants once grown

SUBSCRIBER PERKS

Merch, samples, extra seeds, and more!
